Corporate Overview

A Forward-Thinking, Eco-Friendly Manufacturing Company

Polysky Greentech Private Limited is dedicated to driving the global shift toward a circular economy. Headquartered in Bilimora (Gujarat, India), we specialize in the production of high-performance, premium-quality Water Soluble Films. Our mission is to replace traditional, single-use plastics with smart, zero-residue biodegradable packaging solutions that seamlessly dissolve in water.

Our Mission

To develop innovative and sustainable packaging solutions that help industries reduce environmental impact while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and performance.

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in sustainable manufacturing through innovation, responsible growth, and environmentally conscious solutions.

PVA / PVOH Technology

Technical Film Solutions

Our water-soluble films represent an innovative alternative to conventional packaging. Designed to dissolve completely in water without leaving harmful residues, these materials support safer chemical handling, improved dosing convenience, and environmentally responsible corporate operations.

Precise Dissolvability

Engineered to disintegrate at precise target temperatures without leaving residue.

Optimal Tensile Strength

High puncture resistance and physical integrity matching traditional plastics.

Chemical Resistance

Excellent barrier against organic solvents, oils, greases, and chemicals.

Market Applications

Key Industries We Serve

Agrochemicals

Pre-measured, unit-dose packaging for pesticides and fertilizers to reduce direct chemical contact for operators.

Detergents & Cleaners

Single-dose liquid or powder laundry pods and dishwasher tablets that improve convenience and dosing accuracy.

Healthcare & Medical

Infection-control water-soluble laundry bags for bio-hazardous textiles, ensuring safe handling.

Textiles & Embroidery

Clean-dissolving stabilizer backings for automated computer embroidery processing with zero post-process residue.

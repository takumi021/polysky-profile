01 / Corporate Overview

Polysky Greentech Pvt. Ltd.

Polysky Greentech Private Limited is a forward-thinking, eco-friendly manufacturing company dedicated to driving the global shift toward a circular economy. Headquartered in Bilimora (Gujarat, India), we specialize in the production of high-performance, premium-quality Water Soluble Films.

Our mission is to replace traditional, single-use plastics with smart, zero-residue biodegradable packaging solutions that seamlessly dissolve in water. Polysky Greentech is built on the pillars of cutting-edge technology, strict environmental compliance, and customer-centric innovation. Operating from our state-of-the-art facility in the GIDC industrial hub of Bilimora (Navsari, Gujarat), we cater to global business demands for reliable, sustainable, and highly customizable packaging materials.

02 / Technical Film Solutions

PVA / PVOH Technology

Water-soluble films represent an innovative alternative to conventional packaging materials. Designed to dissolve completely in water without leaving harmful residues, these materials support safer chemical handling, improved dosing convenience, and environmentally responsible corporate operations.

Precise Dissolvability

Engineered to disintegrate at precise target temperatures and specific durations without leaving residue.

Optimal Tensile Strength

High puncture resistance and physical integrity matching traditional linear low-density plastics.

Chemical Resistance

Excellent barrier attributes against organic solvents, oils, greases, and concentrated raw chemicals.

Zero-Residue Performance

Breaks down completely into harmless organic compounds, preserving delicate ecosystems and aquatic life.

Environmentally Responsible Packaging

Directly targets global microplastic elimination goals by replacing long-chain single-use materials.

High Performance Solutions

Seamlessly runs on high-speed form-fill-seal (FFS) automated machinery systems.

Why Water Soluble Films?

  • ✓ Secondary waste reduction
  • ✓ Landfill containment and diversion
  • ✓ Secure toxic chemical isolation
  • ✓ Precision dose normalization
  • ✓ Continuous form-fill-seal automation support
